Principles

These are practical principles that I have developed through experience in building software.

  • The domain is the most complex aspect of a software system
  • Prefer evolutionary design and continonus refactoring
  • Software design is about governing complexity
  • AI systems should be build with the same principals as software engineering
  • Logs go a really really long way for observability
  • Unit testing is the foundation of reliability and verification
  • Interface design and boundary code are really important to get right because they will be depended on right away
  • Safety and quality in an AI system is about governing random factors
  • Seperate deterministic and non-deterministic parts of your code and pipeline
